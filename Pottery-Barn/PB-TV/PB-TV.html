TV Mount Information

Our $129 flat rate for TV mounting includes mounting a TV to the wall using a flush mount or tilting mount that you supply.

Full motion mounts are an additional $64.50 charge and we cannot mount TVs on certain types of full motion mounts as they do not meet our child safety standards. For TVs larger than 46”, someone will have to help lift one side of the TV on the wall mount once the mount is secured on the wall (The TVs aren’t heavy, just too bulky for one installer to lift). If 2 installers are required on the installation to lift the TV into place, there is an additional charge of $64.50.

If you would like to schedule an appointment, please let us know your address, phone, what size your TV is and what type mount you have.

The $129 flat fee does not include cord management. We can install cord covers that you provide or we can install a “power bridge” unit that you supply to conceal cords inside interior walls if you have sheetrock walls. The flat fee installing a cord cover is $32.25 and the flat fee for installing a power bridge is $64.50.
